Employment:

In addition to the vital services contributed by volunteer staff, we also employ multiple full-time and per diem EMT's and Paramedics who provide dedicated, professional service for the organization and community at all times. This mix of full-time staff and volunteers is what allows Victor Farmington Volunteer Ambulance to provide unmatched, excellent care for our community each and every day. To recognize the consistent, irreplaceable benefits that our full-time and per diem staff gives the organization and community, we provide compensation through the Victor Farmington Volunteer Ambulance budget. This is yet another reason donations are necessary and greatly appreciated.

If you are interested in future employment opportunities with Victor Farmington Volunteer Ambulance, please compile a resume and cover letter and email it to us at info@victorfarmingtonambulance.org!
