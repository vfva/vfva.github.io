Patient: Privacy Practice

Victor-Farmington Volunteer Ambulance Corps, Inc. provides medical transportation and other related services for the community. Due to the nature of the services we provide, we are required to maintain the privacy of certain confidential health care information, known as Protected Health Information, and to provide you with a notice of our legal duties and privacy practices to respect your Protected Health Information.
