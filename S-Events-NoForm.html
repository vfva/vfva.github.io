Services: Special Event Standby

We respond to many special events for standby services including Stuarts International Horse Trials, the Ganondagan Festival, Victor Central School District events, and during the holiday season at East View Mall. At these events, which typically require at least one ambulance to be present, we provide a crew to cover the potential risk of increased call volume. To request a standby, your event must be within the Town of Farmington or Victor.
