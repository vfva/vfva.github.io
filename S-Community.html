Services: Community Services

Meeting Room Use
Our facility's meeting room is available for use by local employers, associations and other not-for-profit organizations that reside within the towns of Farmington or Victor. The meeting room may be scheduled when not in use by the Victor Farmington Volunteer Ambulance staff for events such as training or membership meetings. With the facility, you are welcome to use our presentation technology, which includes two monitors and a projection screen to play videos or to display your presentation from a computer. The room can accommodate approximately 50 people. To schedule a date, you should send a written request 30 days prior to the event date, sent to the attention of the Director of Training.

Equipment Loan
Our equipment loan closet is available to residents of the Towns of Farmington and Victor. Equipment such as wheel chars and crutches may be borrowed at no cost.

Blood Pressure Checks
Blood pressure checks are available at our ambulance base from 9AM-11PM at no cost.
