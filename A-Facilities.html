About Us: Facilities and Vehicles

Vehicles
We currently have 4 Type III Braun ambulances identified as 3391, 3392, 3393 and 3394 as well as two additional emergency response vehicle identified as Medic 66 and 3361. Each of our ambulances and Medic 66 are equipped with Intermediate and Advanced Life Support (ALS) equipment. The ALS program includes Class III controlled substance medications commonly used for pain management, cardiac issues, and seizures. The ALS program has achieved additional accreditation to perform Rapid Sequence Induction (RSI) procedures in the field. This program has added additional advanced airway skills and equipment including surgical airway.
