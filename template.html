About Us: Overview

Mission
Striving every day to provide exceptional emergency medical service to the community with highly-trained personnel and an ongoing commitment to exploration, evaluation and utilization of the latest technological resources.

Services
In 1970, Victor Farmington Volunteer Ambulance Corps, Inc. was formed to serve a critical function in the preservation of life for the sick or injured. Since then, we've fulfilled this duty by providing a variety of services to best serve our community including:

• Emergency Response • Training
• Special Event Standby • Community Services

With a commitment to grow with our community, we provide these services for nearly 23,000 residences amidst a population growth of 38% in Victor and an expected 1,000 additional homes in Farmington. Please see our services section for a more in-depth look on all of the services we provide.
